Publications

My research focuses on the engineering of complex, long-lasting, software-intensive systems within various application domains in an integrated approach with different disciplines. I have experience in software language engineering, the engineering of digital twins, and the model-driven software engineering of information and assistive systems. Please see further publications in my Google scholar or Researchgate profile.

  • My Habilitation thesis at RWTH Aachen University, Germany was about “Model-Driven Engineering of Digital Twins with Informative and Assistive Services”.
  • My PhD thesis in the Application Engineering group (Prof. Heinrich C. Mayr) at Alpen-Adria Universität Klagenfurt, Austria, was about cognitive modelling for assistive systems.
